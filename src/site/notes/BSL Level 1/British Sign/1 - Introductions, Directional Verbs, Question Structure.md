# Grammar
## Directional Verbs
Start and End positions of phrases indicate subject/object
- This removes the need for pronouns
- Akin to SVO structure
- i.e. you help me vs me help you

If asking a question, the subject can be at the start or end of the phrase
- i.e. 'you family have' OR 'family have you'

## Q&A Structure
### Questions
Topic - Subject - Question
i.e. Deaf hearing - you - which?

### Answers
Proper form repeats the Q before answering
i.e. Deaf hearing - me - which - hearing
